Returns and Refunds

Have questions about our return policy and refunds? Check out the frequently asked questions below to find answers to common inquiries related to returns.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of the purchase date. The item must be unused, in its original packaging, and accompanied by the receipt or proof of purchase. Please review our complete return policy for more detailed information.

How do I initiate a return?

To initiate a return, please contact our customer support team or visit the returns section on our website. Provide them with the necessary details and they will guide you through the return process.

Are returns free?

Yes, returns are free of charge. We will provide you with a prepaid shipping label to return the item. However, please note that in some cases, return shipping fees may apply for international returns.

How long does it take to process a refund?

Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it and process the refund within 5-7 business days. The refund will be issued using the original payment method.
