Secure Payments

We prioritize the security of your payment information. Learn more about our secure payment process by browsing through the frequently asked questions below.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) as well as PayPal for secure online payments. You can choose your preferred payment method during the checkout process.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we prioritize the security of your payment information. We use industry-standard encryption technology and follow strict security protocols to ensure the confidentiality and integrity of your data during the payment process.

Do you store my payment information?

No, we do not store your payment information. All payment transactions are securely processed by our trusted payment service providers. Your payment details are handled directly by them and are not accessible to our system.

What security measures do you have in place?

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your payment information. This includes encryption, secure socket layer (SSL) technology, and compliance with Payment Card Industry Data Security Standards (PCI-DSS) to ensure a secure payment environment.
